About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer with a strong can-do attitude and passion for learning new languages, frameworks, and technologies.

My expertise lies in JavaScript, React, Redux, and Ruby on Rails. Using these skills I created a comprehensive single page application, beyondPixels, where the users may discover and share the highest quality of photography. The application’s data is stored via PostgreSQL and I connected the Rails backend to AWS S3 for increased security and performance when uploading or accessing the photos.

I also had a chance to work on a team MERN stack project, ReBond as a front end lead. The project was built using Mongoose, MongoDB, Express.js, Node.js, and React. During the project, I had an opportunity to get familiar with SASS and Materialize which were used to design a fully dynamic and responsive UI.

Currently, I am finalizing a survival game developed using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

With experience in Quality Assurance, my programming philosophy focuses on writing code with a critical mindset, calculating all the edge cases. My passion for photography and my UI reviewing/testing experience at a Fortune 100 tech company in Cupertino benefit me in engineering beautiful UIs with modern and user-friendly solutions.
